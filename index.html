Who We Are

We bring expert handyman services directly to your doorstep. Our mission is to provide reliable, efficient, and high-quality home repairs and maintenance, wherever you are. Our business model focuses on personalized service, transparent pricing, and a commitment to exceeding your expectations. We understand that your time is valuable, so we travel to you, equipped to handle a wide range of tasks, from minor fixes to major projects. Trust JPL Home Services to keep your home in top condition, no matter where life takes you.

What We Do

We provide a comprehensive range of handyman services, addressing everything from minor repairs to larger home improvement projects. Our skilled team is ready to tackle your to-do list, ensuring your home is safe, functional, and looking its best.

Landscaping

Includes garden maintenance, shrub trimming, and general yard upkeep.

Painting

Flooring

Installation and repair of various flooring types, including tile, laminate, and hardwood.

Siding & Roofing

Bathroom Renovation
